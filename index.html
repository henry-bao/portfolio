Project

Vade

Role: Lead Developer

Vade is a multipurpose Bot that serves over 300k users and plans to support every feature possible. Our development team aimed to improve the user experience on the platform with bot interactions. We introduced features such as reaction buttons and auto-fill commands to address the lack of accessibility for users with disabilities that might experience problems interacting on the textual-based platform. The tech stack we utilized primarily consisted of TS/JS, Node, MongoDB, and Git.

Time: December 2020 - Present
